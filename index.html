About Our Village

A Place With Rich History

Kolappuram has preserved its historical charm while embracing modern comforts. Our village is known for its traditional architecture, friendly community, and sustainable living practices.

Kolappuram is a place within Abdul Rahiman Nagar Panchayat in Malappuram district, Kerala, India. It's located near National Highway 17. The road leading to Calicut International Airport begins from Kolappuram. Nearby areas include Mampuram and Tirurangaadi.
